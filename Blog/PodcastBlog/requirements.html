Project Background

In response to the growing demand for something fresh and personalized in the podcast world, our project tackles the challenge users face when seeking new and user-friendly content. We're developing an AI-driven podcast that makes use of Artificial Intelligence to create text, audio, and video content for our project. In this journey, guided by John McNamara (IMB), our IXN industry project mentor, we recognize the vast potential within the somewhat undiscovered realm of AI podcasting, where our innovative approach promises to unravel exciting possibilities and reshape the way listeners engage with content.

Project Objectives

Our project aims to create an innovative AI-driven podcast platform, with a primary focus on leveraging artificial intelligence technologies to enhance the podcasting experience. It will be web-based, providing users with seamless accessibility and engagement.

Moscow Requirement List

We categorized our project requirements into four groups: must-have, should-have, could-have, and won't-have, providing a structured approach to prioritize and manage our project goals.

  • MUSTs

    Use of appropriate music (intro, outro)

    Podcast Structure (intros, greetings, meaningful filtered information)

    Text to Speech (generate text based on a topic or description and then use text to speech tool, eg ElevenLabs)

    Functioning and not overloading UI (easy to use and navigate)

    Interactive elements for episodes (play, forward, volume etc buttons)

    Implement a scheme where a recent video (or article) can be transcribed to create a new episode on the topic of that source

  • SHOULDs

    Detailed episode descriptions

    Suggestions on topics (users could type a topic that they would like an episode to cover)

    User Accounts (for recommendations. Users can have existing episodes recommended to them based on what they have liked and listened to)

    Help Page (explaining how to navigate the tool)

    Episodes Search (Based on keyword or types)

    Transcriptions and translations

    Diverse range of guests and topics

    Simulations of interviews with historical figures

    Saved episodes feature

  • COULDs

    Comedic elements in the episodes (the user can select the style of the episode whether its comedic, formal, entertaining etc)

    Use of relevant stats or graphics (eg if the episode covers a recent sport match, there can be a relevant graph to show some interesting data)

    Immersive 3d audio

    Light/Dark mode

    Download Episodes

    Share options

  • WONT'HAVEs

    Augmented Reality

    Website Translation
